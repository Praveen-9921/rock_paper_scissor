STONE PAPER AND SCISSOR

RULES OF THE GAME

1. You have three options: Rock, Paper, or Scissor.

2. You and your opponent both pick one option.

3.You both have the 3 second to pick one out of three

4. If you both pick the same option, it's a draw.

5. If you pick stone and your opponent picks paper, you lose. But if your opponent picks scissor, you win.

6. If you pick paper and your opponent picks scissor, you lose. But if your opponent picks paper, you win.

7. If you pick scissor and your opponent picks stone, you lose. But if your opponent picks paper, you win.
